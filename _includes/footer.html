About this Site

This project uses the Digital Analytics Program code developed by 18F's. We acknowledge and are grateful to 18F for open-sourcing this work.

This data comes from an unified Google Analytics account for City & County of San Francisco web pages, created by the SF Digital Service & Products Team, offers important insights around how people are interacting with local government online. This dashboard serves to help government agencies understand how people find, access, and use city-services online. It does not track individuals and anonymizes the IP addresses of visitors.

The vast majority of City & County of San Francisco websites are represented in this data, as the San Francisco Digital Service & Products Team currently collects web statistics from 100+ City websites, across multiple agencies, departments, and offices.

If you have any suggestions, please contact the San Francisco Digital Service & Products Team.

As part of the public domain, the original analytics.usa.gov website and its content available without cost or restriction. You can find the code for theanalytics.usa.gov website and the code for 18F's Analytics Reporter for data collection on GitHub.
