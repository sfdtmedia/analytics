About this Site

This data provides a window into how people are interacting with the government online. The data comes from a unified Google Analytics account for City & County of San Francisco web pages created by San Francisco Digital Service & Products Team . This program helps government agencies understand how people find, access, and use government services online. The program does not track individuals, and anonymizes the IP addresses of visitors.

Not every City & County of San Francisco website is represented in this data. Currently, the San Francisco Digital Service & Products Team collects web statistics from around 50+ City & County of San Francisco websites including many agencies, departments, offices.

This open source project uses the Analytics Reporter code developed by 18F's, and is in the public domain, which means that this website and its data are free for you to use without restriction. You can find the code for this website and the code behind the data collection on GitHub.

If you have any suggestions, please contact the San Francisco Digital Service & Products Team.
